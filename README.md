# skybound-runner
🌤️ Skybound Runner
A retro-styled 2D side-scrolling platformer built with Godot Engine. Race through obstacle-filled worlds, collect ancient sky coins, and restore light to your homeland!

📖 Story
The protagonist hails from a small village under a dark, stormy sky. To restore light to their homeland, they must ascend the Skybound Path — racing through forests and ruins while collecting ancient sky coins to power the Beacon of Dawn.

🎮 Gameplay

Traverse continuously-scrolling terrain
Jump and dodge obstacles (rocks, barrels, stumps)
Land on platforms and breakable stone blocks
Collect coins to increase your score
Reach the level end or survive the set time to win — collisions with hazards reduce health or trigger game over
